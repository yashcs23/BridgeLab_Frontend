Silver

Silver is a precious metallic element with the chemical symbol Ag and atomic number 47. It is a soft, shiny, white metal known for its exceptional lustre, ductility, and conductivity. Silver is the best conductor of heat and electricity among all metals, which makes it highly valuable for industrial and technological applications.

Gold

Gold is a precious metal with the chemical symbol Au (from the Latin word aurum) and an atomic number of 79. It is one of the most valuable and admired elements known to humanity for its bright yellow color, resistance to tarnish, and high malleability.
