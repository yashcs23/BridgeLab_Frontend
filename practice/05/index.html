Free

Freedom is a fundamental human right that signifies the state of being able to act, speak, and pursue one's aspirations without unnecessary external constraints or coercion. It is a deeply personal concept whose exact meaning varies for every individual, but it universally represents the power to make independent choices and live a life aligned with personal values

Silver

Silver is used for jewelry, decorative items, and as an investment, but it is also a vital industrial metal for electronics, solar panels, and medical applications. Its high electrical and thermal conductivity makes it ideal for circuit boards and electrical contacts, while its antimicrobial properties are used in medical devices and water purification.

Gold

Gold is formed through a two-part process: its creation in deep space via supernova and neutron star collisions, followed by its concentration in Earth's crust through geological processes like hydrothermal activity and weathering. In the cosmos, the intense heat and pressure of these violent events created gold atoms, which then reached Earth on meteorites.
